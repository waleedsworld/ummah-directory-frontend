Ummah Directory started in 2003 as a widely-used printed catalogue featuring Muslim owned businesses across Melbourne.
In 2024 we realised that most people are searching online for just about everything.
There is no single portal that allows Muslim consumers to access Muslim and Halal businesses across the spectrum, whether it be Halal Food, services, tradies and more.
Our Mission at Ummah Directory (“Ummah” being the Arabic word for community) is to create a hub for Muslim users.

We want to create a portal that leverages each other and enriches both the Muslim and the wider Australian community at large.
We aim to create a positive and thriving community ecosystem that supports one another through commerce and engages with one another with the highest levels of integrity.
